About

Servicing Christ by Serving you Well

  • 2018

    Our humble beginnings

    After working as a developer for 15 years, founder Paul Randall conceived a vision for a Front End Development consultancy that would partner with top Dev shops filling a critical gap between full stack developers and UX Designers. Indy Dev Services was launched.

  • 2019

    A Consultancy Is Born

    After only 6 months, founder Paul Randall reorganized and began accepting contracts with Indianapolis area Dev Shops.
